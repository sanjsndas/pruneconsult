Pruning & Plant Health Tips

  • Prune your trees during late winter to early spring before buds open for healthier growth.
  • Always use clean, sharp tools to avoid damaging plant tissue and spreading disease.
  • Remove dead or diseased branches immediately to prevent the spread of pathogens.
  • Avoid excessive pruning in one season as it can weaken the tree and reduce fruit production.
  • Regularly inspect your plants for signs of stress, pests, or disease to catch problems early.
