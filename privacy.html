Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Welcome to PruneConsult’s Privacy Policy. At PruneConsult, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services and website. We collect several types of information in order to provide and improve our services.

We collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you register or book a consultation. When you visit our website, we may also collect data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referral URLs to analyze usage and enhance user experience. We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, log usage patterns, and assist with marketing efforts. You may control cookies through your browser settings.

Your personal information is used to provide pruning and plant health consultation services efficiently; to communicate with you about your bookings and inquiries; to analyze website usage and improve its content and functionality; to send promotional materials, updates, and newsletters with your consent; and to comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud or unauthorized activities.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our business, such as hosting companies and email services, solely to improve our services. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure servers to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. While we strive to safeguard your data, no online system is completely secure.

You have the right to access and obtain a copy of your personal information, to request corrections or deletions of your data, to restrict or object to our processing of your data, and to withdraw your consent where applicable. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email below.

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies when you visit them. Our services are not directed to individuals under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under this age. If you believe we have accidentally collected such data, please contact us immediately.

We reserve the right to update this policy as needed. We encourage you to periodically review this page for any changes. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please contact us at info@pruneconsult.com or by phone at +1 877-292-3344.
